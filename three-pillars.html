PIPE-WORKS

Three things hold. Everything else leaks.

The press is down. Most of the paper’s wet. This was printed anyway.

Things are attempted. Hands move. Pressure builds. Sometimes something comes out the other side.

If nothing passes through, nothing changes.

Work does not happen nowhere. It happens in rooms, cellars, taverns, borrowed spaces with bad drains.

The water was already there.

Some things are remembered by people. Other things must be written down so nobody gets to pretend later.

If it’s uncomfortable, keep it.

Printed while raining.
Dried near the boiler.
Filed anyway.
